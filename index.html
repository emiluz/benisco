About Us

Benisco was started by Susan Davis, as a casual neighborhood place to get a bite to eat and a glass of wine. We are a family run restaurant with a simple menu and a big heart. We started small, and have grown to a nice place for the neighborhood. As time goes by we will be increasing our menu, our hours, and service to the surrounding community.

Located at the NE Lloyd hub, Benisco is a neighborhood restaurant with a rotating menu that sources the freshest ingredients available.

Stop in for our signature dishes and weekend brunch.
